Process

How It Works

1
Sender Registers Cargo

Create an account, fill in receiver details and destination. A unique tracking code and QR is generated instantly.

2
Desk Officer Records Payment

Sender presents the code at the station. Desk officer scans, records payment, and unlocks loading.

3
Driver Starts Trip

Loaded cargo is assigned to the trip. GPS tracking begins automatically as the bus departs.

4
Cargo Arrives & is Delivered

Destination staff marks cargo as delivered. An OTP is sent to the receiver via SMS automatically.

5
Receiver Picks Up Securely

Receiver presents OTP or QR code. Staff verifies and releases cargo. Property marked picked up. Done.
