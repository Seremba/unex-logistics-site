Account Management

Delete Your Account

Request deletion of your UNEx Logistics account and associated data

⚠️

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to your cargo tracking history, registered properties, and all account data. Please make sure you want to proceed before submitting a request.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your UNEx Logistics account, contact our support team using any of the methods below. Include your registered phone number so we can identify your account.

  • 1
    Contact our support team via email or phone using the details below.
  • 2
    Include your registered phone number and full name in your request.
  • 3
    Our team will verify ownership of the account before processing the deletion.
  • 4
    Your account will be deleted within a reasonable period after verification. We will confirm via SMS or email when the deletion is complete.

What Data Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted when your account deletion request is processed:

Data Type Action
Account profile (name, phone number) Deleted
Password hash and authentication data Deleted
Pending OTPs and verification codes Deleted
Session and device data Deleted
Cargo and shipment records May be retained*
Payment records May be retained*
Audit logs May be retained*

* Certain records may be retained where required for legal, financial accountability, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, or regulatory compliance purposes. Retained records will be anonymized where possible.

Partial Data Deletion

If you wish to delete specific data without deleting your full account — for example, removing a registered property or updating your contact details — please contact our support team with the specific request. We will assist you in managing your data appropriately.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion, data handling, or your privacy rights under UNEx Logistics, please review our Privacy Policy or contact our support team directly.
