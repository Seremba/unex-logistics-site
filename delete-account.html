UNEx Logistics — Account Deletion

This page explains how users of UNEx Logistics can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

To request account deletion, please contact our support team using the registered phone number connected to your account.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an account deletion request to our support team.
  2. Include your registered phone number and account name if available.
  3. Our team may verify ownership of the account before processing the request.
  4. Approved deletion requests are processed within a reasonable period.

What data will be deleted

  • Account profile information associated with the user
  • Stored contact details linked to the account
  • Other personal account data that is no longer required for operations

What data may be retained

Certain records may be retained for a limited period where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, financial accountability, or audit purposes.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion or data handling in UNEx Logistics, please contact support using the details above.
